About FridgeToPlate

Our Mission

FridgeToPlate was created to help reduce food waste and make cooking more accessible. By identifying ingredients you already have and suggesting recipes that use them, we help you make the most of what's in your fridge.

How It Works

  1. Capture: Take a photo of ingredients in your fridge or pantry
  2. Identify: Our AI recognizes the ingredients automatically
  3. Discover: Browse recipe suggestions based on your ingredients
  4. Create: Try fusion recipes that combine different cuisines

Technology

FridgeToPlate is built with Python using the Flask web framework. We use Google Cloud Vision API for ingredient recognition and Spoonacular API for recipe suggestions.
